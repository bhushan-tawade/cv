The roman reigns

Leati Joseph "Joe" is better known by the ring name Roman Reigns, is an American professional wrestler and former American football player.

He is currently signed to WWE on the SmackDown brand, where he is the current and longest-reigning WWE Universal Champion in his second reign and the current WWE Champion in his fourth reign. Considered one of the best wrestlers in the world,[8][9] his current championship reigns make him WWE's undisputed world champion, referred to within the company as the Undisputed WWE Universal Champion.
